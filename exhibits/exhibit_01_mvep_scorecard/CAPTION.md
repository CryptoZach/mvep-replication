# Exhibit 1: MVEP scorecard

**Consolidated per-product MVEP verdicts across the ten categories and the recursive Cash Settlement Asset companion test.**

The scorecard synthesizes the per-product verdicts of record in A2 PAPER.md into one matrix: ten category rows plus the Cash Settlement Asset companion-test row (the Y axis: the ten MVEP categories plus the recursive Cash Settlement Asset companion test, which the Section 4.8 summary table breaks out as a distinct axis) by fourteen product columns. The figure and the source CSV share this orientation (category rows by product columns), so existing row-number citations into `mvep_scorecard_data.csv` remain valid. Reading the matrix at a glance reproduces the paper's three headline patterns: every U.S.-law Box 2 product in the sample fails Category 1 (Rights Parity) on the Frisch and Dalrymple defect except JPMD, whose closed-network banking context earns a Partial, and the offshore USYC comparator fails it on the absence of any published rights-parity analysis under its governing offshore law; the tokenized funds (BUIDL, OUSG, BENJI, USDY) carry the densest of-record coverage because Section 5.4 Table 4 assesses them across all ten categories; and the recursive Cash Settlement Asset row is where products that would otherwise look comparable separate, because every product whose redemption routes through USDC inherits USDC's failure.

## Legend and sources

Verdict levels: **Pass**, **Partial**, **Ambiguous**, **Fail**, **Not disclosed**, **N/A**, and **Unassessed**. Three of these are easily conflated and are semantically distinct: **Not disclosed** is an assessed verdict (a documented search found no published issuer artifact for the category); **N/A** is an assessed verdict of structural inapplicability (the category has no object for the product structure; no rendered cell currently carries N/A (the prior sole instance, BENJI Category 8, was corrected to Fail in the 2026-06-17 audit on finding a live on-chain freeze and clawback surface on the registered fund's Stellar issuer account, which falsified the no-on-chain-admin-keys premise)); **Unassessed** means no of-record verdict exists because no assessment was performed (rendered `--` through 2026-06-11; relabeled to the word "Unassessed" 2026-06-12 to remove the visual ambiguity with Not disclosed). The Ambiguous and Not-disclosed levels are carried from Section 5.4 Table 4's richer vocabulary; the Section 4.8 summary table uses the four-level Pass/Partial/Fail/N-A scheme, mapped here without re-grading.

Each assessed cell carries a source suffix:

- **T2** = Section 5.4 Table 4 (full ten-category assessment for BUIDL, BENJI, OUSG, USDY).
- **SUM** = the summary table (Category 1, Category 6, and the Cash Settlement Asset companion test for the nine-product sample).
- **F5 / F6** = Findings 5 and 6 (Tier 2 custody/reconciliation/insolvency cells; DAI's operational-category cells).
- **WE** = the per-product worked examples in the Supplementary Materials.
- **NEW** = USYC offshore comparator scoring; see caveat.
- **NEW2** = USD1 GENIUS-era comparator scoring; see caveat.
- **JX** = derived from the Exhibit 2 Category 5 jurisdiction matrix (`exhibit_02_cat5_jurisdiction_matrix/`) under its scoring rules; the Category 5 row's verdicts of record.
- **CP** = coverage-completion scoring: the nineteen previously-unassessed operational, disclosure, collateral-operability, and recursive-settlement cells, graded on the framework method (on-chain verification for governance; the documented-search standard for reorg and disclosure artifacts) plus each product's documented structure, with one external verification (USDY redemption). Per-cell basis in the coverage-completion table below.
- **RWA** = grounded in the rwa.xyz platform snapshot (the scale source of record), which also supplies the scale line in each product row label (market cap or AUM; `| N h` = per-product holder count where tracked; JPMD is a permissioned bank deposit token rwa.xyz does not track).

## USD1 caveat (GENIUS-era comparator)

USD1 (the World Liberty Financial-branded GENIUS-era stablecoin) is **not** in the nine-product sample but is worked through in full as a comparator; it is scored across all ten category cells plus the Cash Settlement Asset companion-test cell at full worked-example depth. It is treated as a **GENIUS-era comparator** because it is the largest dollar stablecoin rwa.xyz classifies under the GENIUS Act framework ($4,463,980,768 per the 2026-06-11 snapshot; the rwa.xyz GENIUS classification is the platform's taxonomy, not an operative licensure status) and the largest product issued through the federally chartered trust-bank structure the GENIUS framework anticipates (the product itself predates the Act's July 2025 enactment, launching March 2025 and crossing $2 billion by May 2025). The issuer and redeemer of record is BitGo Bank & Trust, N.A., an OCC-chartered uninsured national trust bank (Charter 25366; conditional approval December 12, 2025, converting BitGo Trust Company, Inc., a South Dakota trust company; the launch-era issuer through early December 2025 was BitGo Technologies, LLC, an MSB). All ten category cells plus the Cash Settlement Asset companion-test cell are graded, on three basis classes: structural (the regime-independent Category 1 defect the paper establishes for every Box 2 product, and the Category 6 defect it establishes for every Box 2 fiat-backed stablecoin in the sample), platform-documented (the rwa.xyz snapshot row; same-day Blockscout and CoinGecko pulls), or verified against the product's primary documents and on-chain state. Political-entity risk associated with the issuer brand is out of scope for this structural scoring (CLII-class metrics do not capture it).

**Cell derivations (2026-06-12).** Six initially-scored verdict letters held and the five remaining cells were graded on primary-document and on-chain review: Category 2 AMBIGUOUS (records at multiple layers; no authoritative-record designation); Category 7 NOT_DISCLOSED (documented search; no response plan); Category 8 FAIL (on-chain verified no-timelock upgradeable proxy; bare-EOA mint and freeze keys); Category 10 AMBIGUOUS (liquidation-path inventory performed; neither pass-condition disjunct documented as satisfied, while no scenario demonstrably forecloses all paths); Cash Settlement Asset PARTIAL (documented fiat termination through the regulated trust bank; the USDG/PYUSD pattern; later corrected to FAIL per the B7 CCSA adjudication 2026-06-29, confirmed).
